about our Conference

Greetings and best wishes from International Conference on Science, Technology, Engineering and management, at KIT-Kalaignarkarunanidhi institute of technology Coimbatore, India. We are extremely glad to write to you that we are organizing the Third International Conference on Science, Technology, Engineering and management (ICSTEM’19) on 22 & 23 March 2019 at KIT-Kalaignarkarunanidhi Institute of Technology, Coimbatore, India.

International Conference on Science, Technology, Engineering and Management (ICSTEM’19) aims at bringing together leading academicians, scientists and researchers to exchange and share their experiences, new ideas, views, thoughts and research results in various aspects of Science, Technology, Engineering and Management.
